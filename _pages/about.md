I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Journalism and Communication at Guangxi University and a master's supervisor in both the School of Journalism and Communication and the School of Artificial Intelligence. I received my Ph.D. in Journalism and Communication from Zhejiang University, with joint doctoral training at the University of Wisconsin–Madison.

I conducted postdoctoral research at Hong Kong Baptist University and have been a Visiting Scholar at the Wee Kim Wee School of Communication and Information, Nanyang Technological University.

Research Interests
======

- New media and intelligent communication
- International communication, communication effectiveness, and image construction
- Media trust, misinformation, and AI-generated misinformation governance
- Computational communication
- The social implications of intelligent media
- AI-enabled social science research and education

Research and Publications
======

My research has appeared in CSSCI- and SSCI-indexed journals, including *Contemporary Communication*, *Global Journal of Media Studies*, *Chinese Journal of Network Communication*, *Computers in Human Behavior*, *Health Education Research*, and *Communication and the Public*. I have also presented research at international conferences including ICA, AEJMC, and IAMCR.

My work has been reprinted in *Social Science Digest* and the *China Journalism and Communication Education Yearbook*. I have contributed to a nationally recognized high-quality course textbook and currently lead several provincial/ministerial- and departmental-level research projects while participating in national and provincial projects.

Teaching and Mentoring
======

I supervise undergraduate innovation and entrepreneurship projects, freshman Student Innovation Training Program (SITP) projects, and student career-planning projects. Student teams under my guidance have received national- and provincial-level awards in advertising, creative communication, and storytelling competitions.
